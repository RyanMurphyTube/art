Les fenêtres
Murphy's debut exhibition showcases a number of oil on canvas works created during the last three and a half years. From flowery, impressionist window studies, to minimal, abstract figurative works, 'Les fenêtres' offers a joyful viewing experience through the artists' use of bright, bold colours, and simple, playful forms. The exhibition also features a virtual reality installation, created by Murphy, which places the viewer inside the exhibited works. Using this technology, Murphy has begun to explore how traditional oil paintings can be expanded upon and experienced in a 3-dimensional, digital space.
